Join the Alumni Community for a Special Weekend


con·viv·i·um

kənˈvivēəm/

  • Latin 1: A banquet, a party, a feast.
  • English 1: A symposium.
  • English 2: (ecology) A geographically isolated population of a species that shows differentiation from other populations of the same species; becomes a subspecies or ecotype.

Derived from Latin, 'con-' (together) + 'vivere' (to live).

Convivium is an in-person gathering where Minerva alumni from all cohorts come together to celebrate our shared experiences and forge new memories. It is a space for us to disconnect from the world to reconnect with one another. Fully volunteer-driven, it is an event meant for alumni by alumni. And, we may be biased, but it is absolutely magical!

Details


Location

Convivium 2024 will happen at Pocono Springs Retreat Center in Pennsylvania, USA. You can read more information about the venue on their website.


Dates

May 16-19, 2024.


Attendees

Only Minerva University (and MSKGI) alumni – graduating 2023 or earlier – are invited to this event.


Cost

The ticket price is $525 per person. $200 deposit is due when filling the registration form, and the remaining balance is due January 15th, 2024. For information on what is included in the ticket price, see the FAQ.
