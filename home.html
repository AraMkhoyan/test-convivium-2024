What?

Convivium is a Minerva reuniun event organized by Alumni Concil. During the event alumni from different classes get together to catch up with old friends, get to know their former classmates as adults, play, laugh, and enjoy a few days away from their daily respnsibilities.


Where?

This year the Convivium will happen at Poconos Retreat Center in Pennsylvania, USA. You can read more information about the venue on their website


When?

May 16-19, 2024.


Who?

Only Minerva University (formally known as Minerva Schools at KGI) alumni are invited to this event.
