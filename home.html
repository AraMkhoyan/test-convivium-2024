Join the alumni community for a special weekend


Convivium 2024 will take place at Pocono Springs Camp from May 16 to 19, 2024. All graduates from the classes of 2023 and prior are invited to attend.

Details


What?

Convivium is a Minerva reunion event organized by Alumni Concil. During the event alumni from different classes get together to catch up with old friends, get to know their former classmates as adults, play, laugh, and enjoy a few days away from their daily responsibilities.


Where?

This year the Convivium will happen at Poconos Retreat Center in Pennsylvania, USA. You can read more information about the venue on their website.


When?

May 16-19, 2024.


Who?

Only Minerva University (formally known as Minerva Schools at KGI) alumni – graduating 2023 or earlier – are invited to this event.


How much?

The ticket price is $500 per person, due when filling the registration form. Early registrations submitted by September 1 23:59 PT are $50 off for a total of $450 per person.

Contact

If you have any questions please contact us at convivium2024@gmail.com
