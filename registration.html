    • All refunds should be requested via email - convivium2024@gmail.com

    • Applications are filled out within the registration form and are due October 1

    • Depending on the aid received, the final full payment is due January 15.

    • Any payment for either installment above stated amount for your category is considered a donation (for example, paying $300 deposit for a regular room by October 1 will include $200 deposit fee and a $100 donation)
