Important Policy Information

  • Eligibility
    • This event is exclusive to alumni who graduated from Minerva University (Minerva Schools at KGI) in 2023 or earlier. Any current students or former students who dropped out are not eligible to attend this event and will not be refunded the deposit if paid.
  • General payments
    • Registrations after October 1 are considered waitlist and not guaranteed. We will accept sign ups as the capacity allows. If registration is not approved, payment will be refunded in full.
    • Ticket payments should be processed before submitting this form. You will be asked for the confirmation number or other identifying transaction information on the form.
    • You will receive a confirmation email once the team has received and verified your payment.
    • The full ticket price is US$525 (please see the FAQ for what the ticket includes).
      • We are offering $25 off your final payment ($500 total) due January 15 if the initial deposit is paid and this form is submitted by September 4, 2023 11:59PM Pacific Time. If you are eligible, this information will be included in the confirmation email.
    • Payments can be made in one or two installments.
      • The first deposit payment is due October 1st. Please see the Regular/Single room policies below for the deposit amount due October 1st.
      • A separate form will be sent to every eligible attendee on December 15th. This will be used to submit the final amount, which is due January 15th.
      • You can pay for the full ticket price in one installment by October 1st if you are not applying for financial aid or signing up for a single room.

  • Regular Rooms
    • The deposit amount for regular shared rooms is $200 due October 1st.
    • The final payment of $325 is due January 15th ($300 if the Early Bird discount is applied).
  • Single Rooms
    • The ticket price for single rooms is $775, and there is no Early Bird discount applicable to single room requests.
    • We have a very limited number of single rooms, and so requests for one are not guaranteed.
    • The deposit amount for single rooms is $525 due October 1st.
    • The final payment of $250 is due January 15th (no Early Bird discount is available for single room requests) if the request is approved.
    • If your single room request is rejected, the $525 ($500 before September 4 11:59pm PT) deposit will be considered as your full ticket price for a regular room.
    • Single room requests are not eligible for the full payment option by October 1st due to the possibility that the request would not be approved.
  • Refund Policy
    • All refunds should be requested via email to convivium2024@gmail.com.
    • Full deposit refunds are available if requested by October 1st.
    • Requests made between October 1st and January 15th are eligible for refunds worth half the total payment value received.
    • No refunds are available if requested after January 15th.
    • Donations are not eligible for refunds (some special circumstances subject to the team's consideration).
  • Financial Aid
    • Applications are filled out within the registration form and are due October 1st.
    • Applicants may be contacted by the team while the decision is pending for clarifying questions.
    • Applicants will get an email confirmation of their financial aid decision between October 1st and January 15th.
    • Depending on the aid received, the final full payment is due January 15th.
  • Donations
    • Any payment for either installments above the stated amount of your chosen room/payment option is considered a donation (for example, paying a $300 deposit for a regular room by October 1st will include the $200 deposit fee and a $100 donation).
    • Donations will be used to cover Financial Aid needs and any extra costs required for the event.
